Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The short version: We do not collect any personal information from you or your children. No accounts, no logins, no forms. We only use anonymous visitor counts to understand how many families are using this course. That is it.

Who We Are

Digital Duty Challenge is a free, family-friendly cyber awareness course created by Cybernym.io, a Botonym.com company. Our mission is to help families have open, informed conversations about digital safety.

Website: challenge.cybernym.io

What Information We Collect

We keep this as simple as possible. Here is exactly what we do NOT collect:

  • No names, email addresses or contact details
  • No account registration or login required
  • No payment information
  • No children's personal data
  • No quiz answers or course results stored on our servers

Your children's answers and scores exist only in your browser during the session. Once you close the page, nothing is saved or sent anywhere.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to count how many people visit our course. This helps us understand whether the course is reaching families and how we can improve it.

Google Analytics collects anonymous information such as:

  • Approximate country or region (not your exact location)
  • Type of device used (phone, tablet, laptop)
  • How long visitors spend on the course
  • Which browser you are using

Google Analytics uses cookies ‚Äî small files stored in your browser. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Cookies

Our course uses only the cookies set by Google Analytics as described above. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or any cookies for marketing purposes. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from completing the course.

Children's Privacy

This course is designed to be used by families together. We take children's privacy very seriously.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children
  • Children are not required to provide any information to use this course
  • No child's quiz responses are stored, transmitted or shared
  • We comply with the UK Children's Code and GDPR requirements for services likely to be accessed by minors

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade or share your data with any third parties for commercial purposes. The only third party involved is Google via Google Analytics as described above.

Your Rights (UK and EU)

  • Ask what data we hold about you (we hold none beyond anonymous analytics)
  • Request deletion of any data
  • Opt out of Google Analytics tracking
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO at ico.org.uk

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy we will update the date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection practices that compromise your or your children's privacy without clearly communicating this.
